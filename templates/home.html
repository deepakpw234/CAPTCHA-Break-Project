CAPTCHA Break

This website is designed to convert CAPTCHA images into text format.
Users can upload CAPTCHA images and receive the corresponding text.
The CAPTCHA images are sourced from the Election Commission of India website.
The prediction method used here is Random Forest, which achieves an accuracy rate of 99%.
